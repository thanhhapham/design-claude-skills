# Carousell Product Context

## What Carousell Is
A C2C (consumer-to-consumer) marketplace where people buy and sell secondhand and new items. Operates across Southeast Asia and East Asia — primarily Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, Indonesia, Hong Kong, and Taiwan.

## Our Users
- Mobile-first. Most users are on Android mid-range devices.
- Mix of casual sellers (decluttering) and professional resellers.
- Buyers browse habitually, often without a specific intent (discovery-driven).
- Price sensitivity is high — negotiation is a core behaviour.
- Trust is a significant barrier; users are cautious about scams.

## Apps They Use Daily
Evaluate designs with reference to the patterns users already know from:
- **Shopee / Lazada** — primary e-commerce reference. Users expect fast-loading image grids, bottom tab navigation, prominent price display, seller ratings, and chat-based support.
- **TikTok Shop** — video-forward discovery; users are used to content-commerce hybrids.
- **Facebook Marketplace** — casual C2C; familiar with DM-to-negotiate flow.
- **WhatsApp** — chat is the primary communication tool. Users expect messaging to feel instant and lightweight.
- **Grab / Gojek** — familiarity with map-based UI, ratings, tipping/confirmation flows.
- **Instagram** — visual browsing, story-format content, explore grid.

## Carousell Design Principles to Apply
- **Trust signals matter** — seller reputation, verified badges, response rates, and listing completeness affect conversion. Flag where trust signals are weak or missing.
- **Chat is core** — the offer/negotiation flow via chat is a primary conversion path. Design should support quick, low-friction chat initiation.
- **Price is a first-class element** — price, discount, and negotiability should always be prominent and clear.
- **Listings are image-driven** — photo quality and composition affect click-through. UI should support images, not compete with them.
- **Low-literacy and multi-language users** — avoid jargon. Labels, CTAs, and error messages should be plain and direct. Some users read in Bahasa, Filipino, or Chinese.
- **Speed over elegance** — users on lower-end devices expect snappy interactions. Heavy animations or complex layouts may hurt perceived performance.

## Common User Goals
- Sell something: list quickly, get offers, close fast.
- Buy something: find it, verify it's legit, negotiate, pay.
- Browse: discover deals, wishlist items, follow sellers.

## Using Premium and Western App References
Premium and top-tier app design (Apple, Airbnb, Linear, etc.) is a valid reference for quality bars — interaction polish, typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy. Reference these when relevant.

However, apply the trade-off lens: Carousell users' daily experience is shaped by Shopee, Lazada, WhatsApp, and TikTok Shop. A pattern that feels intuitive on a premium Western app may feel unfamiliar or friction-heavy to our users. When suggesting something that deviates from SEA app norms, acknowledge that gap and reason through whether the quality gain is worth the familiarity cost.
