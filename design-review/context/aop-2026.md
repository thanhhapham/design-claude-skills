# Carousell AOP 2026 — Annual Operating Plan

*Source: Carousell BU AOP document, Oct 2025. Target: 30.4M GP, 300k EBITDA, 268 HC.*

---

## Strategic Context

Carousell is shifting from horizontal growth to category-market depth. The strategy is to double down on categories with strong PMF rather than grow horizontally. Competition is intensifying from FB Marketplace, Goofish (HK/SG), Mercari (HK/TW/MY), TikTok Shop, and Xiaohongshu. AI-led discovery is reshaping SEO/SEM.

**2025 lessons that shape 2026 design:**
- Supply is recovering (esp. H&T +43% SG YoY) but demand is down 10–20% YoY
- Price competitiveness vs. Taobao/Shoppee is a real barrier — pricing UX is critical
- Trust investment works: HK phishing scams dropped 87% after coordinated action
- Smaller, focused teams ship better than multitasking ones

## MWB Pillar 1: Category Market Focus

### Goal 1: Home Cluster SG & HK — #1 Priority

**Targets:**
- Responses: SG 482k → 531k (+10% YoY) by Jun 2026; HK 215k → 235k (+10%)
- SA renewal count: 50% → 75% (+50% YoY) by Jun 2026
- SA $ renewal: to 100% by Jun 2026
- Home Services monthly avg responses: 66.8k → 80k (+20%) by Jun 2026
- Respondents: 32k → 38.5k by Jun 2026
- MAU to Search Conversion: +20% YoY

**What's being built:**
- "Home+" tab on home and category screens — one-stop for homeowner needs (Renovation, Aircon, Cleaning, Moving, Furniture, Appliances)
- Provider-oriented search/discovery (not listing-oriented — buyers search for providers, not listings)
- Service Provider Profiles/Business pages
- MyHome improvements: reno/repair price estimation, better service request submission
- Lead recycling and cross-category nudges
- ROAS model evolution: moving toward Pay-per-view / auction model
- SEO/AEO: Home Svcs 3.5K → 10.5K, Furniture 14K → 43K sessions/month

**Design implications:**
- Any design reducing friction for buyers to find or contact service providers moves this needle
- Trust signals for service providers (response rate, reviews, verification) are conversion-critical
- Provider profile completeness is a key ARPU driver (affects SA renewal)
- The service seeker experience must feel like searching for a person/business, not scrolling listings
- Impression distribution: higher paid tiers should get better search placement

### Goal 2: Autos SG — Supply Growth

**Targets:**
- Direct-owner supply: +30% (weekly effective listings 153 → 170 by Jun 2026)
- Dealer base: +20% (1,000 dealer listings in Carousell Select by Mar 2026)
- MY: Cars live listings 23k → 118k (+513%) via Mudah cross-listing

**What's being built:**
- Carousell Select: curated flow with verified direct owners + reputable dealers only
- Motorcycles, Car Rental, Accessories & Workshops: ARPU growth 7–26% target
- MY: Cross-listing API with Mudah

**Design implications:**
- Carousell Select needs clear differentiation from standard listings — verified badge, curated presentation
- Trust signals for Autos are unique: mileage, condition, ownership history, dealer verification
- Direct owner supply is scarce vs SgCarMart — discovery UX must work harder with fewer listings
- Natural search / smart refinement reduces hard-filter constraints, enabling more results shown
- See `context/autos.md` for full Autos-specific design context

**Targets:**

- Buyer trust signals (authentication, inspection workflow communication) drive response rate
- See `context/luxury.md` for full Luxury design context

### Goal 4: Toys & Games SG & HK (Q2/Q3)

**Context:** T&G is Carousell's #1 volume category. SG: 28% of listings, 17% of responses. HK: 43% of listings, 32% of responses. Competition from Mercari (HK), TCGUnite (SG), Collektr (MY).

**Targets:**
- PRIV T&G listings: SG 1.24M → 1.68M (+35%), HK 2.13M → 2.66M (+25%)
- Responses: SG 4.6M → 6.2M (+35%), HK 6.3M → 8.5M (+35%)

**What's being built:**
- Vertical-specific filters & facets: Grading, Rarity, Series, Character, Release Year, Region, Edition
- List with AI for collectibles: auto-detect card/figure name and series
- Pricing history guides and thematic feeds (e.g. "Pokémon TCG Mega Decks", "Retro Figurines")
- eBay cross-listing pilot for global TCG supply

**Design implications:**
- Filter UX for T&G must support collector-specific attributes (rarity, grading) not just generic filters
- List with AI reduces listing friction — quality and speed are the dual wins
- Thematic feeds are a discovery surface, not just a category page — editorial curation matters
- Price history as a trust signal (vs. eBay, StockX references)

## MWB Pillar 2: Horizontal Marketplace Growth

### Goal 5: Buyer Experience (BX)

**Targets:**
- Engaged buyers: +25% by Dec 2026
- Average Buyer Action Points: +2 by Dec 2026
- SPC/MM/MP CTR: 0.27% → 0.33%
- Push/EDM CTR: 10% → 12%

**What's being built:**
- 2 new owned traffic formats (SPC, MM, MP) with higher reach-to-click conversion
- Agentic CRM via Aampe pilot — personalised, automated journeys per user per channel
- Thematic browse + natural search (LLM-powered, semantic matching)
- Chinese language support for new migrant segments in SG/HK

**Design implications:**
- Anything increasing click-through, reducing decision fatigue, or surfacing compelling listings helps
- Natural search: users describe items in their own language (style, feel, occasion) → system matches
- Thematic feeds: Toys & Games, Furniture, Women's Fashion — discovery without intent
- New audience segment (Chinese expats, Indian/Bangladeshi/domestic workers) — WhatsApp/WeChat-native patterns, trust-first UX

---

### Goal 6: Marketplace Liquidity (STR)

**Targets:**
- SG 7D STR: 13.6% → 16% (+18% YoY)
- HK 7D STR: 12.6% → 15% (+19% YoY)

**Main levers:**
1. **Products & Pricing foundation:** Product matching for 60% of listing views in SG+HK Electronics, 20% Home; track historical transacted prices for fast/normal/slow sale bands
2. **Seller nudges:** Guide sellers to price in the fast-sale band; measure listings priced-to-sell
3. **Buyer nudges:** Reduce low-ball offers below fast-sale range
4. **Good Deals badging:** Auto-identify listings ≥S$60/HK$300 that are ≥40% savings vs brand new on Shopee → in-product and marketing comms
5. **Chat conversion:** Guide buyers to better offers, sellers to better decisions, using pricing + sellthrough data

**Design implications:**
- Compelling prices = #1 lever for liquidity — pricing UX is not optional
- Good Deals badge needs high visual salience (Von Restorff Effect) — must stand out without being noisy
- Seller nudge in the listing journey (pre-, during, post-sell form) — timing and framing critical
- Price signals in LDP, SERP, Explore Tab, Chat — multi-touchpoint
- Chat is the primary conversion path — offer/negotiate/confirm flow must be supported, not just initiated
- Design inspiration references: StockX (price transparency), GOAT (offer flow)

## MWB Pillar 3: Trust & Safety

### Goal 7: Fraud Reduction

**Targets:**
- SG: eCommerce fraud cases 120/month → 60/month (-50%)
- HK & MY: -50% from current base
- Moderation decisions understandable from reason alone: 25% → 90%
- Appeals citing "unclear reason": 42% → 7%
- Avg account review handling time: 5.55 min → 2.5 min (Lodestar AI)

**What's being built:**
- Lodestar: AI-assisted triage, routing, summarisation for fraud moderators
- Moderation reason taxonomy overhaul — clearer feedback to users, reduces appeals
- 100% machine-readable decisions

**Design implications:**
- Any design making scam vectors obvious or making verified sellers/listings visually distinct contributes
- Error messages and moderation feedback must be in plain language — Nielsen H9
- Self-serve resolution flows reduce CS load (Goal 8 dependency)
- Verified badges, Carousell Select, Certified labels are all trust infrastructure

---

### Goal 8: CS Efficiency

**Targets:**
- Annual CS professional fees: $1.6M → $1.1M by Dec 2026
- Monthly CS support fees: $135k → $90k

**What's being built:**

- Emily (Agentic CS) handling more tickets

**Design implications:**
- Self-serve resolution flows, clear error states, better help documentation reduce support load

## MWB Pillar 4: Recommerce Growth

### Goal 9: Buy Button & Shipping

**Targets:**
- SG WF BP transactions: 5k → 10k/month (+200%) by Jun 2026
- SG BP transactions: 18.8k → 24k/month (+28% YoY) by Dec 2026
- Carousell Official Delivery monthly txns: 36k → 54k (+50%)
- Buy button enabled listings: 450k/month (47% enablement) → 550k/month (+20%)
- SG cancellation rate: 35% → 25%

**Shipping context (critical):**
- Current: J&T from S$4.90, Singpost Drop-off to Doorstep from S$2.10
- Target: SPX integration at S$1.60 (D2D + drop-off, no label printing)
- Also exploring: S$1 PUDO option, Ninjavan SG↔MY cross-border at S$3–4, eBay cross-listing

**What's being built:**
- SPX integration (cheapest shipping = #1 needle mover, learnt from Vend)
- Seller Drop Off Lockers / Buyer Collection Lockers (SingPost + SPX)
- KYC improvements: notifications, reminders, better Stripe error surfacing
- Payout without KYC for casual sellers (direct PayNow)
- Partial escrow removal for trusted sellers
- Chat → Buy Button pathways (currently not natural transition)
- Order cancellation reduction: seller guidance, item unavailability features
- Consistent transactional comms across app, notifications, email

**Design implications:**
- Shipping cost and convenience are the #1 conversion driver — surface them prominently
- KYC friction is killing enablement — simplify or remove where legally possible
- Seller friction reduction = more BP-enabled listings = more buyer options
- PayNow payout path for casual sellers addresses the biggest segment barrier
- Chat-to-Buy-Button transition needs a designed pathway, not just a hope that users will tap it
- Cancellation reduction: seller guidance at the right moment (not retrospective)

## Cross-Cutting Themes (Apply to Any Review)

| Theme | What it means for design |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **Pricing transparency** | Price is a first-class signal. Always prominent, always honest. Price ranges, Good Deals badges, pricing guidance should never be hidden or secondary |
| **Trust signals** | Seller reputation, response rate, verified badges, listing completeness affect every funnel. Missing trust signals = lower conversion, always |
| **Listing quality** | Photo quality, title clarity, condition disclosure drive click-through and STR. List with AI addresses this on the supply side |
| **Speed** | Mid-range Android on 3G — perceived performance as important as actual. Skeletons, optimistic UI, image lazy-loading all matter |
| **Chat as conversion** | Offer/negotiate/confirm via chat is Carousell's primary conversion path. Anything making it faster or lower friction helps every pillar |
| **Natural search** | LLM-powered semantic search is in-flight — designs that reduce rigid filter dependence align with this direction |
| **New audiences** | Chinese expats + migrant workers in SG/HK — WhatsApp/WeChat-native, organic/community-driven channels, trust-first |
